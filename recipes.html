This is a popular recipe for the traditional and original Burek

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef

  2. 1 tablespoon ground allspice

  3. 1 tablespoon paprika

  4. salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

  5. 1 medium potato, finely chopped

  6. medium onion, chopped

  7. 16 sheets phyllo dough, thawed

  8. Â¼ cup melted butter







Directions:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

Step 2: Cook and stir ground beef in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain fat. Stir in allspice, paprika, salt, and pepper. Transfer mixture to a large bowl and stir in potato and onion.

Step 3: Stack two sheets phyllo dough on a work surface. Spoon 1/8 of the beef mixture into a tube to make a coil, Repeat to make seven more burek

Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 20 to 30 minutes.
